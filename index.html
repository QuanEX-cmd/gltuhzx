2. Article with Audio: Guide to Renting a Studio

Step 1 Make a budget
Set your monthly rent budget according to your salary and living cost after graduation.

Step 2 Choose location
Pick a studio near your workplace, subway station or convenient shopping areas.

Step 3 Check house condition
Inspect water, electricity, network, furniture and safety before signing the contract.

Step 4 Sign rental contract carefully
Read all terms clearly, confirm deposit, rent period and maintenance rules.
